************************************************Exam Management System************************************************

CLASSES

1.Student : Manages Operations of Students  Data
2.Faculty : Manages Operations of Faculty Members
3.Course  : Manages Operations of Students Course Data  
4.Paper   : Manages Operations of Student Paper
5.Date    : Manages Operations of Paper Date
6.Time    : Manages Operations of Time Of Paper
7.Marks   : Manages Operations of Exam Result

Instance Variables

1.Student : name , regno , semester , age , program , gender , institution , email , mobileno , UserName , Passcode , Address...
2.Faculty : teachername , department , hiredate , birthdate , facutyaccount , faculty office , description...
3.Course  : coursename , coursecode , course description , coursesemester , courseprogram , courseregstration , course student...
4.Paper   : papertitle , papercode ,  examtype ...
5.Date    : papertime...
6.Time    : paperdate...
7.Marks   : student name , student result , paperName , totalmarks , marksdescription , percentage , grade , marksofexamType(e.g final or mid)...

Methods

1.Student : setname() , setregno() ,setsemester() , setage() , setprogram() , setgender() , setinstitution() , setemail() , setmobileno() , setUserName() , setPasscode() , setAddress()...
2.Faculty : setTeacherName() , setDepartment() , setDateOfHire() , setDOB() , setAccount() , setOffice() , setTeacherInfo() or setDescription...
3.Course  : setCourseName() , setCourseCode() , setcourseInfo() , setSemester() , setCourseProgram() , setCourseRegistration() , setCourseStudent()...
4.Paper   : setName() , setPaperCode() , SetTypeOfExam()...
5.Date    : setDate(Day , month , Year having its conditions)
6.Time    : setTime(hour , minute, seconds having its condtion)
7.Marks   : setStudentName() , setStudentResult() , setPaperTitle() , setMarks() , setMarksDescription() , setPercentage() , setGrade() , setExamMarksType()....
